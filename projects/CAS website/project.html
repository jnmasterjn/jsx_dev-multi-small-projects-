What is a CAS project?

The CAS project is a significant part of the CAS program. It involves collaborative and hands-on experiences that extend over an extended period. The project provides students with the opportunity to demonstrate initiative, demonstrate perseverance, develop new skills, and engage with issues of global significance. CAS projects can take various forms, including organizing events, creating art, participating in community service, undertaking physical challenges, and more.
